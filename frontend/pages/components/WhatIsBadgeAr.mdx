#### What is Badge.Ar?
BadgeAr is a place where you can transform your awards, trophies, and badges into a digital token (NFT).

Currently, we support minting [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/) achievements,
meaning that you can create NFTs from your gaming sessions.
